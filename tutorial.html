In order to configure secure access to .bit domains in Chromium/Chrome, you must perform the following steps. You must perform these steps before the installer will continue.

  1. In Chromium/Chrome, visit chrome://net-internals#hsts
  2. Under “Add domain”, enter the following: domain “bit”, STS unchecked, PKP checked, fingerprint "sha256/AAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAA=".
  3. Click “Add”.
